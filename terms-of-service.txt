Terms of Service for "CMExpert", a classical music robot classifier.

1) Users are defined as the ones who complete the (google) form
   that is used as a human-benchmark for comparison for the 
   current robot. 

2) Completing the form is optional

3) The public-domain songs are being made public in this "public 
   Github Project" under the ".mid" (MIDI) format

4) The logistic regressor script is algo being made public in this repo

5) Besides the educational potential of the scripts here made public, 
   and the educational potential of the public comparison against humans 
   made with the ANONYMOUS DATA processed from the associated google-form, 
   no benefit whatsoever is offered to "Users" defined at (1)
